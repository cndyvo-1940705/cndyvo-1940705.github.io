Welcome To UnRavel!

UnRavel is a company dedicated to help you untangle your yarn! All the knitters and crocheters, the feeling of excitment when stepping into your local yarn store is unmatched. There are so many different colors and fibers and it can get overwhelming.

So, you come home with your brand new hanks and you're about to wind them up into nice center-pull cakes. Five minutes later, you're sitting in a tangled mess of yarn!

At UnRavel, we do the work you don't want to so you can get started crafting. How does it work, you ask? Click the button below for more details!

How Does It Work?

Well, it is quite simple! Just bring us your tangled mess and we'll get our UnRavel experts to untangle your yarn. The people who work for us love this kind of stuff, so let us work so you don't have to!

How much will it cost? We calculate the cost depending on the weight of the yarn. A standard 100g hank will cost $5.
